Chinese Dancer Ziyan

A collection of dances — from traditional and classical to modern — performed by Ziyan【紫颜】— Mainland Chinese dancer.

Ziyan is the Bilibili Dancing Festival 2018 (BDF2018) Choreography Competition Award Winner, for her dance Youth Tour《少年游》set to Journey to the West《大话西游》

Ziyan is the Bilibili Dancing Festival 2021 (BDF2021) Best Individual Award Winner, for her dance Flowers and the Moon《花月成双》to the song with the same name by Sanwu Marblue【三无Marblue】

For a long time, the Chinese style dance at Station B [Bilibili] has been a niche. It is precisely because of the silent efforts of Ziyan and other Chinese style dancers up, and even the power of love, the audience of Chinese style dance has gradually increased. Of course, it is also due to the contribution of Henan Satellite TV. The dance variety show Dancing for a Thousand Years has brought traditional Chinese dance into the public eye.

It’s never a ceiling, it’s just your best audience. For Ziyan, if she is just satisfied with the ceiling of the dance area, she will not be able to break through herself and create better dances. As the best audience for your own work, if you are not satisfied with it, how can you satisfy other audiences? The light in the heart is the gaze that looks at everything you love. It is this love for dance that supports Ziyan to constantly innovate. It is this light in the heart that drives all walks of life to move forward and promotes the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation!

If Chinese style dance is the light in Ziyan’s heart, then Ziyan is the light in my heart. My life for Ziyan. Pay tribute to Ziyan and all the light chasers of the times [Come on]

— Gong Shangjiao Zhengyu, admirer, 2022-01-05
