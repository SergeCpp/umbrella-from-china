Chinese Dancer Xiaodan

A two-and-a-half-hour collection of sixty dances — from traditional and classical to modern — performed by Xiaodan【晓丹】— Mainland Chinese dancer.

Xiaodan is the Bilibili Dancing Festival 2020 (BDF2020) House Dance Award Winner, for her dance Rainbow Beat《彩虹节拍》to the song with the same name by Zhao Fangjing【赵方婧】

Xiaodan is the Bilibili Dancing Festival 2021 (BDF2021) Best Individual Award Winner, for her dance Sunward《向阳》to the song with the same name by Yaorenmao【咬人猫】

Xiaodan is the Bilibili Dancing Festival 2022 (BDF2022) Best Individual Award Winner, for her dance Shining Echo《闪耀回响》to the song with the same name by Chen Leyi【陈乐一】
