Liu Keyi Songs

Liu Keyi【刘珂矣】— Since the release of "Banhu Yarn" and "One Sleeve Cloud", high-quality singing and songwriting works, widely acclaimed, the "Zen Chinese Style" she created has also become the most unique music label in recent years. In January 2021, she was awarded the "Best National Style Artist" award at the 12th Chinese Golden Melody Awards. (Baidu Encyclopedia)
