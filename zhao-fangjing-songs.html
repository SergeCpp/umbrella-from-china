Wei Shi《未时》Sun Side

Wei Shi【未时】— One of the Twelve Hours【十二时辰制】— Corresponds to 13:00 to 15:00 in modern time. At this time, the sun dipped and began to tilt westward, so it is also called the side of the sun and the reflection of the sun. (Baidu Encyclopedia)
