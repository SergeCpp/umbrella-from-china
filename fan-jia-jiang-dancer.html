Chinese Dancer Fan Jia Jiang

A little one-hour collection of fifteen dances — from traditional to ballet and modern — performed by Fan Jia Jiang【凡茄酱】— Mainland Chinese dancer.

Fan Jia Jiang is known as the great admirer of Luo Tianyi【洛天依】— famous Chinese virtual artist — so her dances are often either set to Luo Tianyi’s songs, or she cosplays Luo Tianyi in them.
