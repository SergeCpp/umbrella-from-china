Chinese Dancer Miao Pu Jiang

A little hour-and-a-quarter collection of about twenty dances — from traditional to ballet and modern (including Chinese Style Jazz) — performed by Miao Pu Jiang【喵扑酱】— Mainland Chinese dancer.

The first Miao Pu video I ever knew about was Drop Pop Candy — the one that got me hooked. The moment I saw it, I was attracted by her brisk and energetic dance steps, which gave me the feeling of a poised, elegant young lady. I silently collected many of her original works, and every time I watch her video, I watch it till the end all the way through, because for me, her house dance videos are always deeply attractive, and at the same time, they give me motivation to keep watching!

— Fan Jia Jiang, dancer
