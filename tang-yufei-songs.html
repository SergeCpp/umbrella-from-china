Tang Yufei Songs

Tang Yufei【唐⁠语⁠霏】formerly Tang Poetry【唐⁠诗】⁠— Singer and lead singer of the band. In 2015, served as the lead singer of the Shiguang Band and released the original song "City Love Chengdu". Participated in and won the Chengdu Division Championship of the YAMAHA Asian Beat Band Original Works Competition. In 2016, won the national championship of Angela Chang Imitation Show in Dragon TV's "The Hidden Singer".
