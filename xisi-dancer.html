Chinese Dancer Xisi

A tiny half-hour collection of just seven dances — from traditional to modern — performed by Xisi【西四】— Mainland Chinese dancer.

Xisi is the Bilibili Dancing Festival 2021 (BDF2021) Best Individual Award Winner, for her dance Flowers and the Moon《花月成双》to the song with the same name by Sanwu Marblue【三无Marblue】
